Yuki Tabata

Yuki Tabata is the brilliant mind behind the hit series Black Clover . He is well-known in the industry for his incredible work ethic and his ability to draw high-energy action sequences that keep readers engaged. Tabata often draws inspiration from his own life struggles to fuel the theme of perseverance seen in his main character, Asta. His unique art style and fast-paced storytelling have made him one of the most respected creators working for Weekly Shonen Jump today.

Anime that I like.

Black Clover ☘
A fast-paced fantasy shonen anime/manga about Asta, a determined boy born without magic in a world where it is everything. Alongside his rival Yuno and the Black Bulls squad, Asta strives to become the Wizard King using rare anti-magic abilities. It features intense action, magic, and themes of perseverance.
Daily Life of Immortal King
Is a Chinese donghua (animated series) following Wang Ling, a near-omnipotent, nonchalant high schooler with immense, seal-constrained powers. He seeks a quiet, normal life, but effortlessly solves major, comical supernatural threats while trying to keep his abilities hidden from classmates and his "Demon King" title a secret.
Wiseman Grandchild
Is an Isekai anime following Shin, a modern-day human reincarnated into a fantasy world. Raised by the legendary sage Merlin, he possesses immense magical power but zero common sense. To learn socialization, he enrolls in a magic academy, quickly becoming an overpowered, famous, and socially naive hero.
Campfire in Another World
a chill, slice-of-life isekai anime (animated by MAPPA) where salaryman Tsuyoshi Mukouda is accidentally summoned to a fantasy world. His only skill, "Online Supermarket," allows him to order modern Japanese food and goods. Using this, he travels, cooks, and accidentally tames a overpowered, gluttonous Fenrir named Fel.
