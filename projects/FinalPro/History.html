Origin

Gundam started in 1979 and became one of the most influential anime series. within the mecha genre, featuring giant robots called "Mobile Suits" used in warfare. Kira yamato is the main character of Gundam SEED, one of the most popular series in the franchise. as of 2026, Gundam has over 50 years of history with numerous series, movies, and model kits.
