Biography

Jesus Villamor was one of the most famous Filipino fighter pilots during WWII. He defended the Philippines against Japanese forces and received the Distinguished Service Cross for his heroism.

Achievements

  • Defended the skies over the Philippines
  • Received Distinguished Service Cross
  • Recognized as a national hero
