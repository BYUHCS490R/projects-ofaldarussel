Service & Contributions

Lieutenant Jose Gozar was a key member of the 6th Pursuit Squadron of the Philippine Army Air Corps. During the December 10, 1941 raid over Zablan Field, his machine guns jammed in the middle of a dogfight.

Rather than retreat, he repeatedly attempted to ram a Japanese bomber, forcing the enemy to flee and saving the airfield from further attack.

Achievements

  • Aerial heroism during the early Japanese invasion
  • Awarded the Distinguished Service Cross for extraordinary bravery
  • Survived the initial battles and the Bataan Death March
