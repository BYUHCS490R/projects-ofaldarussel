Biography & Service

Antonio Bautista was a distinguished Filipino pilot known for his bravery and dedication to the Philippine Air Force post-WWII. He became a symbol of courage and patriotism.

Achievements

  • Renowned fighter pilot
  • Flew the F-86 Sabre jet
  • Bautista Air Base named in his honor
