Panglao, Bohol

Panglao is a charming island in the Philippines known for its pristine beaches, clear waters, and rich cultural heritage. It's a perfect destination for those seeking a tranquil escape and authentic local experiences.

Visitors to Panglao Island can relax on Alona Beach, go island hopping to Balicasag Island, enjoy snorkeling and scuba diving in vibrant coral reefs, and join dolphin watching tours while experiencing its laid-back tropical atmosphere.
