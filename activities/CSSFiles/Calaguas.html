Calaguas Island

Calaguas Island is the kind of place travelers dream about but rarely find. Tucked away in Camarines Norte, it boasts powder‑fine white sand, crystal‑clear turquoise waters, and a peaceful atmosphere untouched by mass tourism. It’s the perfect escape for anyone craving raw, natural beauty without the crowds.

Travelers fall in love with Calaguas for its unspoiled Boracay‑like beaches without the crowds, breathtaking sunsets that wash the sky in gold and pink, the rare chance to camp under a blanket of stars, exciting adventures like island‑hopping, snorkeling, and hiking, and the warm, welcoming locals who make every visit feel personal and unforgettable.
